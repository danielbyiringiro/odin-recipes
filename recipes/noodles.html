Noodles


A bowl of noodles

Cooking noodles is a simple and easy process that yields a versatile ingredient that can be used in many dishes,
such as spaghetti, fettuccine alfredo, and pad Thai. Experiment with different types of noodles and sauces to
create your own unique dishes!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of noodles (such as spaghetti or fettuccine)
  • 4-6 quarts of water
  • 1 tablespoon of salt

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with water and bring to a boil.
  2. Add salt to the boiling water.
  3. Add noodles to the boiling water and cook until al dente.
  4. Drain the noodles and serve with your favorite sauce.
