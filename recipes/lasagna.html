Lasagna


A plate containing lasagna

To prepare lasagna, cook lasagna noodles until they are al dente.In a skillet, cook ground beef with tomato sauce,
garlic, and Italian seasoning. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg, salt, and pepper. Layer the meat
sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella cheese in a baking dish. Repeat until all ingredients
are used up, then bake for 50 minutes at 375°F.

Let the lasagna cool for 10-15 minutes before serving. Lasagna is a hearty and delicious dish that can be made
ahead of time and reheated later. It is perfect for family dinners, potlucks, and other gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar (24 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • container (15 oz) ricotta cheese
  • 1 container (15 oz) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente.
  3. In a skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until no longer pink. Add minced garlic, tomato sauce, and Italian seasoning. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, beaten egg, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Spread a layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  6. Layer cooked lasagna noodles on top of the meat sauce.
  7. Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture on top of the noodles.
  8. Sprinkle a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the ricotta mixture.
  9. Repeat steps 5-8 until all of the ingredients are used up, ending with a layer of mozzarella cheese on top.
  10. Cover the dish with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  12. Let the lasagna cool for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
